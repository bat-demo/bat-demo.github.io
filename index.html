Solutions

1

Autonomy Through Infrastructure (ATI)

Full-Scale Vehicle Logistics

Autonomy Through Infrastructure (ATI) solution is the world's first globally certified, infrastructure-based autonomy platform, fully operational and ready for serial production. It provides comprehensive, fleet-level autonomous driving that automates the entire vehicle journey within automotive factories and large-scale logistics hubs.

This turnkey solution automates complex processes that are difficult or impossible to manage with fixed conveyor belts, offering unparalleled flexibility for modern production.

Proven Capabilities

Manages vehicle movement from the moment they roll off the end-of-line, through various processing stations, to their final parking destination.
Seamlessly navigates vehicles through dynamic environments, including car washes, diagnostic test benches, charging stations, and wheel alignment stands.

Successfully deployed and proven in the real world at three BMW factories in Germany, with further expansions planned across Europe and the U.S.

Proven Future-Ready for
Modular Production

ATI addresses upstream logistics by autonomously transporting a vehicle's "skateboard" chassis to various production islands before the vehicle's own driving hardware is even installed. This supports flexible and modular production architectures, reducing dependency on traditional, linear assembly lines.

2

Autonomous Yard Tractor (AYT)

For Ports and Logistic Yards

Autonomous Yard Tractor (AYT) solution delivers precision autonomous operations, offering the adaptability required in complex logistic environments. Designed for seamless integration into dynamic traffic scenarios—such as busy logistics yards, mixed-vehicle environments, and areas frequently traversed by pedestrians and equipment—the AYT operates safely and effectively in all weather conditions, day or night.

AYT can be further enhanced when packaged with our ATI solution, leveraging smart infrastructure to deliver the highest standards in autonomous driving. By combining tractor-based and infrastructure-based intelligence, this integrated package significantly boosts robustness, efficiency, and reliability.

Technology

At the heart of Seoul Robotics' technology are two synergistic AI engines that enable full-stack autonomy. Our platform combines hyper-realistic perception with intelligent, large-scale fleet orchestration.

Horus™

AI-Powered Perception

The "eyes" of our system, Horus is our patented AI technology that provides superhuman awareness. It fuses and processes data from hundreds of disparate sensors simultaneously to create a single, flawless, high-fidelity 3D model of the operational environment.

Atlas™

AI-Driven Fleet Orchestration

Atlas is our AI-driven planning engine designed to control hundreds of vehicles simultaneously. Unlike traditional deterministic methods that rely on pre-programmed movements, Atlas uses advanced AI to dynamically plot and manage complex, fleet-wide maneuvers in real-time, ensuring optimal flow and efficiency.
